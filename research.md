My current research focuses mainly on Deep Learning models that process sequential, time-series data. I study deep time-series modelling, how to successfully apply neural networks in different forecasting tasks and also how to improve existing methods and create better ones. 

Methodologically wise, I am currently researching the use of attention-based models like Transformers and their variants in time-series forecasting and how to improve them. Application wise, I am currently researching the forecasting of fashion trends and sales in a multi-modal setting.

If you are interested in any of the things mentioned above and would like to collaborate or simply have a chat, don't hesitate to contact me.
