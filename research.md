My current focuses mainly on Deep Learning models that process time-series data. I try to study deep time-series modelling, how to successfully apply neural networks in different forecasting tasks and also how to improve existing methods and create better ones. Methodologically wise, I am currently researching the use of attention-based models like Transformers and their variants in time-series forecasting and how to improve them. Application wise, I am currently researching the forecasting of fashion trends and sales in a multi-modal setting.

Other topics which I am working on and I am very interested in are multimodal learning, graph neural networks and generative models.
If you are interested in any of the things mentioned above and would like to collaborate or simply have a chat, don't hesitate to contact me.
